# Welcome to SNUG! 🎉

We are the **Sofia Nix User Group** — a community of developers, system administrators, and technology enthusiasts in Sofia, Bulgaria who share a passion for [Nix](https://nixos.org/) and [NixOS](https://nixos.org/).

## Join Our Community

Whether you're a Nix veteran or just curious about what Nix can do for you, you're welcome to join!
