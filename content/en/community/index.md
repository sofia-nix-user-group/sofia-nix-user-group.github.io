## 🤝 Join Our Community

SNUG is a welcoming community for anyone interested in Nix, NixOS, and declarative systems. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced Nix user, there's a place for you here!

## 💬 Chat With Us

Join our real-time chat to ask questions, share your projects, and connect with other Nix enthusiasts in Sofia:

**[Join our Discord](https://discord.gg/mjr4nH6U)** — Our primary communication platform

## 📅 Attend Events

We organize regular meetups in Sofia. Check our [Events page](/events/) for upcoming gatherings.

## 🌐 Follow Us

Stay updated with SNUG news and announcements:

- **GitHub** — [sofia-nix-user-group](https://github.com/sofia-nix-user-group)
- **Discord** — [Join us](https://discord.gg/mjr4nH6U)

## 🙋 How to Get Involved

### Attend a Meetup
The easiest way to get involved! Come to our next event, introduce yourself, and meet the community.

### Give a Talk
Have something interesting to share about Nix? We're always looking for speakers! Topics can be:
- Beginner tutorials
- Advanced techniques
- Real-world case studies
- Tool demonstrations
- Migration stories

### Help Organize
Want to help run SNUG? We need help with:
- Finding venues
- Coordinating speakers
- Managing social media
- Welcoming newcomers

### Contribute to This Website
This website is open source! Feel free to suggest improvements or submit pull requests on [GitHub](https://github.com/sofia-nix-user-group/website).
